01

Overview

Vettd is a real estate agent matching service operated by Link Re LLC, a Texas limited liability company doing business as Vettd ("Vettd," "we," "us," or "our"). We connect homeowners, buyers, and investors with screened, licensed real estate agents based on each person's specific situation, location, and goals.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit www.vettdagents.com, submit our match request form, communicate with us by phone, email, or text message, or otherwise interact with our services (together, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

02

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you complete our match request form or communicate with us, we collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Your real estate situation — whether you are looking to sell, buy, sell and buy, or invest
  • Property details you share, such as address, neighborhood, timeline, estimated value, budget, experience level, and current circumstances
  • Whether you have previously spoken with or engaged another agent
  • Your consent preferences for non-marketing and marketing communications
  • Any additional information you share with us in reply to our text messages, emails, or calls

Information collected automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect limited technical information, including your device type, browser type, operating system, IP address, pages visited, referring source, and the approximate time and date of your visit. This information is used to operate and improve the site and is not used to identify you personally.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Match you with a vetted real estate agent in our network based on your situation, market, and goals
  • Contact you by phone, email, or text message to confirm your submission and coordinate your introduction
  • Introduce you directly to a matched agent and provide that agent with the information necessary to assist you
  • Respond to questions, requests, or follow-up communications
  • Send you transactional and service messages related to your match
  • Send you optional marketing communications if you have opted in
  • Improve our Services and internal operations
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

04

SMS & Text Messaging

When you submit our match request form and affirmatively check the non-marketing consent box, you consent to receive text messages from Vettd at the phone number you provided, including follow-up communications related to your agent match.

Key SMS Disclosures

Message frequency: Varies based on your situation and engagement. Typical volume is 1–10 messages per month.

Message & data rates: Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

Opt out at any time: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You will receive a confirmation reply and no further messages.

Help: Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at the information below.

No sharing of mobile data for marketing: We do not share, sell, or transfer your mobile phone number or SMS consent information to third parties or affiliates for the purpose of marketing, promotions, or lead generation. This information is used solely to deliver the Services you requested.

If you also opt in to marketing communications by checking the optional marketing consent box, you may receive occasional market updates, tips, and promotional messages. You can opt out of marketing messages at any time by replying STOP, while still receiving essential service messages related to your match.

05

How We Share Information

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

With matched real estate agents

Once we identify a suitable agent for your situation, we share the information you provided (name, contact details, and situation-specific information) with that agent so they can follow up with you. Matched agents are independently licensed real estate professionals who have agreed to our referral terms.

With service providers

We use trusted third-party service providers to operate our business, including customer relationship management (GoHighLevel), automation (Zapier), voice and text communications (Bland.ai, Twilio, and similar providers), website hosting (Vercel), and email delivery. These providers process information on our behalf and are contractually bound to protect it.

For legal reasons

We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, court order, or governmental request, or if we reasonably believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, enforce our Terms, or protect the safety of any person.

In connection with a business transfer

If Vettd is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to the terms of this policy.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your mobile phone number or SMS consent information with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes under any circumstances.

06

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses a small number of cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use the site. We may also use analytics providers (such as Google Analytics) and advertising pixels (such as the Meta/Facebook pixel) to measure the performance of our marketing.

Most web browsers let you control cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our Services.

07

Data Retention

We retain information you submit for as long as necessary to provide the Services, follow up on your match, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Inactive lead records are typically retained for up to seven years for business and tax purposes, after which they are archived or deleted.

You may request deletion of your information at any time using the contact details below.

08

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws, including:

  • The right to know what personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@vettdagents.com. We will respond within a reasonable period and may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.
