Who We Accept

Not every agent
makes it in.

The Vettd name means something to the consumers we serve. Before we add an agent to the network, we verify that they meet our standards. If you're a good fit, the application process is quick. If you're not, we'll let you know.

  • ✓

    Active real estate license in good standing

    Licensed and active in your state, no current suspensions or pending disciplinary actions.

  • ✓

    Proven transaction history

    Minimum of 10 closed transactions in the last 24 months, or equivalent production history we can verify.

  • ✓

    Clear local market expertise

    Demonstrable knowledge of your target ZIP codes — pricing trends, school districts, neighborhoods, inventory dynamics.

  • ✓

    Strong client reviews and reputation

    Positive online presence — Zillow, Google, brokerage profile. We spot-check and ask for two client references.

  • ✓

    Responsive, professional communication

    We expect partner agents to respond to every introduction within one business hour. Speed protects the consumer — and your conversion rate.

  • ✓

    Willingness to sign a referral agreement per match

    When we identify a qualified lead for you, a referral agreement is sent before the introduction is made. Signing unlocks the connection.

The Application Process

From application
to introduction.

Straightforward, fast, no strings. If you're a fit, you're in the network within the week.

Step 01 · 3 minutes

Submit your application

Tell us about your experience, your market, and your specialties. We only ask what we need to make a real decision — not a data-harvesting form.

Step 02 · 24–48 hours

We review and verify

We personally review your license, production history, reviews, and specialties. If anything needs a quick conversation, we'll reach out — otherwise, you'll hear back by email with a decision.

Step 03 · Upon approval

You're added to the network

Your ZIP codes and specialties are locked in and you become a Vettd partner for your area. No monthly fees, no minimums, no cost to join.

Step 04 · Per match

Match → referral agreement → introduction

When we identify a qualified lead that fits your territory and specialties, we send you a referral agreement. Once signed, we make the introduction and you take it from there.
